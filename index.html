Provisional License Course

A provisional license allows the holder to drive with certain restrictions until they pass their driving test.

Eligibility:
For individuals who are at the initial stage of learning to drive.

Content:
This course covers the basics of driving, understanding road signs, and traffic laws. It prepares students for their first driving experience under supervision.

Price:
$15.00 until you pass your test.

Class 2 License Course

This course is designed to equip drivers with the skills to safely handle medium-sized vehicles and prepare them for the Class 2 license test.

Eligibility:
For drivers who wish to operate medium-sized vehicles.

Content:
The course includes in-class theory on fatigue management, work time, and logbook requirements, as well as practical training in operating a rigid vehicle within the weight range of 6,000kg to 18,000kg.

Price: $7.00/lesson

Learn More

Class 4 License Course

This course is to prepare drivers for the Class 4 license test, enabling them to operate heavy vehicles.

Eligibility:
Aimed at drivers intending to drive heavy vehicles.

Content:
Similar to the Class 2 course, it focuses on larger vehicles, typically over 18,000kg. The course covers advanced driving techniques, safety protocols, and compliance with transportation regulations.

Price: $5.00/lesson
