About Us - The Famous Driving School

Our Story

Famous driving school was established in 2014 with the aim of promoting road safety and providing high quality driving license. It is known for its professionalism and experienced instructors. The driving school was founded by the two brothers, Hobbs Mavhunga and Takudzwa Mavhunga, they are both experienced and highly qualified instructors. Famous driving school offers class 2 and class 4 driving classes for both beginners and experienced drivers. As it started in 2014 it was offering only class 4 driving lessons then in mid-2016 it expanded to class 2. Over these years, Famous driving school has built a strong reputation for producing confident and skilled drivers. It is located in the Harare CBD.
